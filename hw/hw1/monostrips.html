Home Programmable Monostrips AI Solar Roadways



In his article, Hands On With The Visible Energy Programmable Monostrip, on techcrunch.com, John Briggs found companies that have given a powerstrip a built-in server. With this server, you are able to monitor and chart the amount of energy that is pulled through the power strip. Knowing the amount of energy that you are consuming allows you to take control of all the wasted energy. The other useful bit of a server behind a power strip is the ability to turn off your products away from home. Knowing and controlling your power consumption can lessen the amount of energy you waste over time.
