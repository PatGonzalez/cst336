Solar roadways have been in development for the past couple of years, and companies such as SolarRoaways have made interlocking solar panels made of glass that can harvest solar energy can carry the weight of semi trucks. These panels can also communicate across vast distances to control traffic, melt ice, and display road obstruction the moment they happen. According to a report on CNBC.com, Solar roads: The future of clean energy?, Ammar Frangoul reported that Dutch researchers have been converting cycle paths with concrete panels that have solar panels embedded in them.
